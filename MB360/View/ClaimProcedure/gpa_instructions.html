GROUP PERSONAL ACCIDENT PROCEDURE STEPS

Intimation Mail to be sent (within 48 hrs from occurrence of the accident) mentioning (date of incidence, details of accident how? when? & where?)

 Documentation for Death Claims

Claim Form of the Insurance Company duly signed, with Stamp & Medical Report Form (Part II) duly signed by attending doctor with stamp
Death Certificate
Post-Mortem Report
Incident Report
Police Panchnama / F.I.R. etc
Any other supporting documents related to the claim

 Documentation for Temporary Total Disablement (TTD) / Accidental Injury Claims

Claim Form of the Insurance Company duly signed, with Stamp & Medical Reports Form (Part II) duly signed by the attending doctor with stamp
Incident Report (HOW, WHEN & WHERE)
Leave record from Employer
Fitness Certificate from attending doctor
Lab Reports
Any other supporting documents related to the claim
ALL DOCUMENTS TO BE SUBMITTED IN ORIGINAL within 15 days from resuming the duties.
